Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Free Trial
All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No charge during the trial period.
Cancel any time during trial for no charge.

2. Monthly Plans
Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable after the trial period ends.
You may cancel at any time — your plan continues until the end of the billing period.

3. Yearly Plans
Yearly plans may be refunded within 7 days of payment if you have not
exceeded 10 posts scheduled on the platform. After 7 days, no refund.

4. Disputes
If you have a billing issue, contact us at support@solyntraai.com before
initiating a PayPal dispute. We will work to resolve it fairly.

5. How to Cancel
Cancel from your account settings page or by emailing support@solyntraai.com.
